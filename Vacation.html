Vacation 2026

Family adventure of traveling to New York City to get our Swedish passports renewed.

Transportation: We took a flight from our hometown to New York City, which was a smooth and comfortable journey. Upon arrival, we used public transportation and rideshare services to navigate the city efficiently.

Accommodation: We stayed at a cozy Airbnb in Pittsburgh, which provided a break in the driving journey. The apartment was well-equipped and offered a comfortable stay, allowing us to rest and recharge for the next leg of our trip.

Activities: We explored the city's famous landmarks, including the Chelsea Market and Times Square. We also visited the 9\11 Memorial, which provided a rich cultural experience.

Food: We indulged in a variety of local cuisines, from street food to fine dining. The diverse food scene in New York City offered us a chance to try new flavors and dishes.

Consulate visit : We successfully renewed our Swedish passports at the consulate, which was a smooth and efficient process. The staff were helpful and provided clear instructions, making the experience stress-free.

Conclusion: Overall, the trip was a great success. We were able to explore a new city, meet new people, and create lasting memories. The experience was both educational and entertaining, and we look forward to planning our next adventure.
